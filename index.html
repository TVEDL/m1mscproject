The Color Burst Story

Who are we? Artists! What do we want? To choose our paint colors! When do we want it? It's already here!

And thus Color Burst was founded: helping artists customize their color palettes to make their art as unique as can be. Our house proudly sources all-natural, eco-friendly, and non-toxic raw ingredients from all around the world, assembling a vide variety of beautifully-vibrant colors for all artists. We grind the pigments into the finest powder using traditional methods such as mortar and pestle, and bottle them into glass vials to await your arrival.

We wish to offer every artist the freedom to create their very own color palettes, as unique to them as possible: that's why we offer such a diverse choice of colors, from primaries to ochres to metallics. You get to pick your very own to mix and match and customize to your heart's content. The sky's the limit!
